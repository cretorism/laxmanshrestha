KEY INITIATIVES

Pioneering Financial Transparency & Digital Administration

Leadership is defined by accountability. In January 2024, our administration pioneered a comprehensive **Financial Transparency & Public Hearing Program** at Shitala Secondary School. By sharing audits, structural breakdowns, and local education spending publicly, we successfully set a new milestone for public school administration in Nepal.
 Financial Audits

Complete structural school budgeting presented with feedback options from the community ward.
